Welcome to my About Me !

I began the pathway to this journey around April of 2024, I was in eighth grade and it came the time where we had to choose our elective. I didn’t know what to pick at first, however, I saw graphic design and I thought it sounded pretty interesting. So not knowing what I was getting into I chose it as one of my electives since I have always been interested in design and such. I was hoping to get Graphic Design because it sounded really enjoyable and like something I may be able to learn a lot from. And then in ninth grade it was in my schedule, I was really excited and it was amazing and I really enjoyed it. All the projects were very fun and I learned new things with each one! Now I’m in web design which is the next course you can take after being in graphic design. We are currently working on building this website and making it better each day.

Using Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator I have had some experience with curves/levels and other image adjustments, gradient tools, blending images together, using masking tools/layers, making bigger projects with smaller images, colorization, pen tool, logos, and more. I already learned how to be better with the pen tool on Adobe Illustrator, and using different brushes to make beautiful images. As well as learning to mix images together to make whole images. Some of the things I am best at doing include using color balance, levels and curves, and other tools related typically in Photoshop. I am also strong in creating things like logos using Adobe Illustrator.
